HOW IT WORKS

It’s easy. Whether you are a family of 2 or 5 we have you covered! Order until midnight for next day delivery on Monday or Thursday and have your meals between 10 am and 6 pm. All our meals are delivered refrigerated and are designed to last in your fridge for up to 3 days. Our menu items rotate so you can try something new or pick an old favorite. Fresh City Meals also requires no level of commitment, order when you want/need a break from cooking!

STEP ONE

Check the weekly menu and our delivery map to make sure you’re in our current coverage area. If you live close, call us at (617) 477-9043 and we’ll see if we can get there.

Order

STEP TWO

RELAX, WE’RE COOKING. Once we get your order we cook and prepare your meal as you like it. If you have any special dietary needs or requests, just let us know. It’s all fresh and it’s all good.

Order

STEP THREE

NO WORRIES. We safely check and package your meal for delivery in sealed microwaveable or oven ready containers. Everything is sealed tight with instructions for preparation. You’ll get a text message when we are on our way. Set the table.

Order

STEP FOUR

UNPACK YOUR FEAST. Unpack and chill, or heat and serve up to 48 hours later. Average prep time from container to family is about 15 minutes, a little longer if you indulge in a DIY (Do-It-Yourself) Pizza Kit or a GIY (Grill-It-Yourself) BBQ spread.

Order

STEP FIVE

YOU KNOW WHAT TO DO NEXT. Fresh City Kitchen fans are raving about our new homestyle meals. Tell your friends and then place your next order. The more you want, the more we’ll invent. No more worrying about pleasing everyone with just one meal or big kitchen clean-ups.
